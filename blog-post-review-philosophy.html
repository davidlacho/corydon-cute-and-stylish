The Review That Changed Everything

Our Philosophy: Feedback is a Gift

Here's what we believe: every review, regardless of the star rating, is an opportunity to become better hosts.

Could we have been defensive? Sure. Could we have dismissed her feedback? Absolutely. But that would be missing the point entirely.

The Power of Communication (During Your Stay)

We always encourage our guests to reach out with any concerns during their stay. A conversation can solve almost any issue in real-time. A finicky shower knob? We could have walked Kendra through the quirks or sent someone over to help. But we understand that guests don't always feel comfortable speaking up, or sometimes issues only become apparent in hindsight.

Our goal is simple: we want every guest to have a five-star experience. That means being responsive, proactive, and genuinely committed to your comfort. When something isn't right, we want the chance to make it right‚Äîimmediately.

That's why post-stay reviews are so important. They tell us what we're missing and help us continuously elevate the experience for everyone.
