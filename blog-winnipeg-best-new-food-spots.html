The Contenders: 22 Outstanding New Restaurants

This year's competition showcases the incredible diversity of Winnipeg's food scene. From French bistros to Korean BBQ, artisanal bagels to Ukrainian comfort food, there's something for every palate. Here's a closer look at each contender:

1. Aroma Bistro

Aroma Bistro offers a fusion of global flavours in a contemporary setting. Known for its innovative dishes and vibrant atmosphere, it's quickly become a favourite among locals seeking a unique dining experience that blends international cuisines with local flair.

2. BabyBaby

Address: 137 Osborne St.

This trendy spot has garnered national attention, being named among the finalists for Air Canada's 2025 Best New Restaurants list. BabyBaby offers boutique cocktails and shared plates in a whimsical dining room, bringing a fresh take on comfort food with artisanal ingredients and creative presentations.

3. Belle's Kitchen + Music Hall

Combining Southern-inspired cuisine with live music, Belle's Kitchen + Music Hall provides an immersive dining experience that appeals to both foodies and music lovers. Guests can enjoy hearty dishes like smoked brisket and jambalaya while listening to local artists perform, creating a vibrant atmosphere where culinary and musical arts intersect.

4. Bull and Filly

Bull and Filly specializes in hearty, meat-centric dishes with a modern twist. This contemporary steakhouse emphasizes locally sourced meats and farm-to-table practices, offering premium cuts in a rustic-chic ambiance that makes it ideal for both casual dinners and special occasions.

5. Colleen

Colleen focuses on farm-to-table dining, emphasizing fresh, locally sourced ingredients in its ever-evolving menu. The restaurant presents seasonal dishes that highlight the best of what Manitoba has to offer, with an elegant setting that makes it ideal for both casual meals and special occasions.

6. Dave And Laverne's Modern Diner

This establishment reimagines the classic diner experience, offering traditional dishes with contemporary flair. From gourmet burgers to inventive milkshakes, Dave And Laverne's Modern Diner pays homage to classic American fare while introducing modern flavours in a retro-inspired setting.

7. Emparepas Treats

Bringing Venezuelan cuisine to Winnipeg, Emparepas Treats is known for its authentic arepas and vibrant flavours. Their gluten-free corn pockets are filled with a variety of savoury and sweet options, introducing Winnipeg to authentic South American flavours that are both traditional and innovative.

8. Forgotten Flavours

Dedicated to reviving traditional recipes, Forgotten Flavours offers a nostalgic culinary journey through time-honoured dishes. The restaurant pays homage to culinary heritage from around the world, bringing lesser-known dishes to the forefront with a modern flair that tells a story of bygone eras.

9. Grizzly Goose

Embracing a Canadiana theme, Grizzly Goose provides a unique dining experience with decor featuring maple leaves and animatronic bears. The menu includes Canadian classics like Montreal smoked meat and Caesars topped with ketchup chips, offering a rustic gastropub atmosphere that combines craft beers with hearty, wilderness-inspired fare.
