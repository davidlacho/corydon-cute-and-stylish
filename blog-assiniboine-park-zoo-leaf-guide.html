Assiniboine Park day guide: zoo, The Leaf, and nearby paths

📍 Winnipeg Guides • ⏱️ 8 min read • 📅 May 30, 2026

Assiniboine Park combines gardens, zoo exhibits, indoor horticulture at The Leaf, and broad walking paths. It works for half-day and full-day plans with children or mixed-age groups.

Choose your entry point

If the zoo is your main stop, begin there in the morning. If you want quieter pacing, start at The Leaf and move outdoors later.

Zoo pacing

The Assiniboine Park Zoo includes large exhibit zones, so comfortable footwear and water breaks help. Families with strollers usually benefit from planning one indoor reset point around midday.

The Leaf timing

The Leaf is a reliable indoor option when weather changes. Allocate at least 60 to 90 minutes for a full walkthrough including photo stops.
