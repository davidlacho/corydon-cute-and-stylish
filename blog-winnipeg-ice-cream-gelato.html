Winnipeg ice cream and gelato: where to go in 2026

🍦 Winnipeg dining • ⏱️ 8 min read • 📅 May 8, 2026

Summer demand pushes Winnipeg ice cream stands into long lines; winter visitors still find hard-scoop shops open along major corridors. This guide separates heritage soft-serve institutions from small-batch hard ice cream and Corydon gelato.

Bridge Drive-In

Bridge Drive-In advertises two Winnipeg locations: 766 Jubilee Avenue at the Elm Park Bridge and 1680 Main Street. The business traces decades of soft-serve service and lists specialty sundaes such as the Goog on its menu pages. Tourism Winnipeg profiles BDI as a city tradition.

Why go: Soft serve, river-adjacent nostalgia at Jubilee, and a second north-side address for shorter drives from some neighbourhoods.

Chaeban Ice Cream

Chaeban Ice Cream produces small-batch hard ice cream at 390 Osborne Street. Founders publish flavour rotations that reference Lebanese and Syrian ingredients alongside classic bases. Awards from the Royal Agricultural Winter Fair appear in Manitoba press coverage and on the shop's site.

Why go: Dense ice cream, creative flavours, and seating along the Osborne Village corridor.

Eva's Gelato & Coffee Bar

Tourism Winnipeg lists Eva's Gelato & Coffee Bar at 1001 Corydon Avenue for Argentine-style gelato plus espresso. The same guide notes Nucci's Gelati at 643 Corydon Avenue for Italian gelati and pasta. Both sit on the strip guests walk from Stay Winnipeg.

Why go: Gelato within walking distance of Crescentwood guest houses and evening patio traffic.

Seasonal hours: Outdoor-first shops shorten winter schedules. Check each website or social feed the day you visit; spring snowstorms still close picnic seating even when counters stay open.

Planning a frozen-treat afternoon

Pair Chaeban with a walk through Osborne Village shops, or combine Jubilee BDI with Assiniboine Park when you already crossed the Assiniboine River by car. Gelato on Corydon fits neatly between bakery pickups (bakery guide) and dinner reservations.

Book your base: Nights after ice cream runs end cleaner when home is nearby. Reserve Corydon Cute & Stylish for Corydon gelato and quick drives to Osborne or Jubilee.
