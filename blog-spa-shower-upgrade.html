What's New: The Spa Shower Panel System

We're installing a premium 52-inch aluminum shower panel system that's going to transform your shower experience. Here's what makes it special:

Rainfall Shower Head

Imagine stepping into a gentle, even rainfall that covers you from head to toe. Our new rainfall shower head provides a luxurious, spa-like experience that's perfect for unwinding after a day exploring Winnipeg or relaxing before heading out to dinner in the Corydon neighbourhood.

8 Adjustable Massage Jets

This is where the real magic happens. The panel features eight adjustable body jets that you can position exactly where you need them. Whether you want a gentle massage after a long day of walking around The Forks, or need to target specific areas after a workout, these jets provide customizable hydrotherapy right in your shower.

Full-Body Relaxation: The combination of the rainfall shower head and adjustable massage jets creates a complete spa experience. You can use them together for the ultimate relaxation, or choose your preferred setting based on your mood and needs.

Multi-Functional Handheld Shower

Sometimes you need flexibility, and that's where the handheld shower comes in. Perfect for rinsing, cleaning, or targeting specific areas, the handheld shower gives you complete control over your shower experience. It features multiple spray patterns, so you can switch from a gentle mist to a powerful stream with just a twist.

Smart LED Temperature Display

No more guessing if the water is the right temperature! The integrated LED temperature display helps you find your perfect water temperature before you even step in. This smart feature makes it easy to set the ideal temperature for everyone in your group, whether you prefer a warm, relaxing shower or a refreshing cool rinse.

Independent Function Control

One of the best features of this system is that all functions work independently, which means you get optimal water pressure no matter which combination of features you're using. Whether you want just the rainfall shower, just the massage jets, or everything at once, the system maintains excellent pressure throughout.

Premium Materials: The shower panel features a sleek aluminum alloy construction with 5mm tempered glass surface, creating a modern, elegant look that complements our mid-century modern home while adding contemporary luxury.
