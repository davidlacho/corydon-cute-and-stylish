A Winnipeg Institution

Gunn's Bakery has been providing customers with old-country, kosher baking since 1937. Founded by Morris Gunn, a Polish immigrant, the bakery has established a level of quality product and service that has made it one of Winnipeg's most celebrated institutions. Located at 247 Selkirk Avenue in the North End, Gunn's has been a cornerstone of the community for over 85 years.

Famous for their handmade breads, bagels, cookies, pastries, and cakes, it's no wonder their products are the staples of many Winnipeg traditions. Whether you're shopping for family and friends, planning for an event, or just grabbing a snack for the road, they have the product to suit your needs.

Canada's Bread Basket

A New Chapter

In 2019, after more than 80 years of family ownership, the Gunn brothers, Arthur and Bernie, passed the torch to Jon Hochman, a local chef with deep roots in Winnipeg's Jewish community. Hochman has embraced the bakery's legacy, ensuring that the cherished recipes and traditions continue to thrive. This transition represents the best of Winnipeg's community spirit‚Äînew ownership that respects and preserves the traditions that make places like Gunn's so special.
