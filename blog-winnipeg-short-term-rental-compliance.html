What Full Compliance Means for You

Winnipeg's short-term rental bylaw came into effect on April 1, 2024, and requires all hosts to meet specific standards. When you book with us, you can be confident that:

  • We're Fully Licensed: Our property has a valid business license from the City of Winnipeg, which we renew annually to maintain compliance
  • We Meet Safety Standards: Our property undergoes regular safety inspections and meets all health and safety requirements, including fire safety equipment and emergency preparedness
  • We're Tax Compliant: All required taxes are included in your booking, and we properly remit the 6% Accommodation Tax to the city
  • We're Good Neighbors: We operate with the knowledge and acceptance of our neighbors, maintaining positive relationships in our community

Peace of Mind for Guests: When you stay with a compliant host like us, you know you're staying in a property that's legally operated, safety-inspected, and community-approved. This means you can relax and enjoy your stay without worrying about whether your accommodation meets legal standards.

Routine Safety Inspections

Your safety is our top priority. We conduct regular safety inspections of our property to ensure it meets all health and safety requirements. This means:

  • All smoke detectors and carbon monoxide detectors are tested regularly
  • Fire extinguishers are present and up to date
  • Emergency exits are clear and accessible
  • All safety equipment is properly maintained
  • The property is regularly inspected by city officials

Safety First: We don't wait for city inspections to identify issues. Our regular self-inspections help us catch and address potential problems before they become safety concerns. This proactive approach ensures our property is always safe and ready for guests.

The City of Winnipeg's bylaw enforcement team conducts routine inspections of short-term rental properties, and we welcome these visits. Transparent Pricing and Tax Compliance

When you book with us, all required taxes are included in your booking price. We collect and remit the 6% Accommodation Tax to the City of Winnipeg quarterly, as required by the bylaw. This tax helps support local infrastructure, tourism promotion, and community services that benefit both residents and visitors.

You'll never encounter surprise fees or hidden costs related to taxes or licensing. Our pricing is transparent, and all required taxes are clearly included in your booking total.
