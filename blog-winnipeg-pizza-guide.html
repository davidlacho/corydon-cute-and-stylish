Winnipeg pizza: styles, rooms, and takeout in 2026

🍕 Winnipeg dining • ⏱️ 9 min read • 📅 May 10, 2026

Winnipeg pizza splits into three rough camps: wood-fired Neapolitan-influenced dining rooms, thin-crust franchises born in Manitoba, and fast-casual build-your-own counters. Beer drinkers can chain dinner with our brewery guide; this page stays focused on slices and pies.

Pizzeria Gusto

Pizzeria Gusto at 404 Academy Road fires pies in a Valoriani oven imported from Tuscany. Tourism Winnipeg notes evening-focused hours on weekends and a west-facing summer patio. Reservations help on Friday and Saturday when Academy Road fills with neighbourhood traffic.

Why go: Charred crust, sit-down service, and a wine list aimed at sharing plates beyond pizza.

Gondola Pizza

Gondola Pizza dates to 1964 and still promotes its thin-crust recipe from the original Pembina Highway kitchen. Expect pickup-friendly boxes, delivery zones across suburbs, and a flavour profile closer to Canadian prairie pizza than Naples.

Why go: Historical Manitoba chain, consistent crunch, and easy group orders.

Neon Palm Pizza

Neon Palm Pizza sells chef-led rectangular pies with rotating toppings. The website lists ordering windows and pickup logistics; follow posted hours because small kitchens pause service when sold out.

Why go: Creative topping combinations and a focused takeout menu.

Parcel Pizza

Parcel Pizza operates a dine-in room with Detroit-style and round pies depending on the week's menu (verify online). The format suits guests who want salads and natural wine adjacent to pizza.

Why go: Sit-down experience outside the traditional red-checkered Italian-American mould.

Rebel Pizza

Rebel Pizza at 110-245 Vermillion Road runs a quick-fired line where guests choose toppings on multiple crust sizes, including vegan dough options listed on the company site.

Why go: Fast customization after airport runs or big-box shopping in south Winnipeg.

Corydon guests: Several Corydon-adjacent kitchens rotate seasonally. From Stay Winnipeg, Academy Road and downtown slice shops sit within a short drive; walking-distance pizza shifts block by block as leases change, so map your route the same morning.

Lodge near the strip: After dinner, walk home to Corydon Cute & Stylish instead of hunting late-night parking downtown.
