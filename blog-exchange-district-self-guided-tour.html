Exchange District self-guided tour: architecture, coffee, and murals

📍 Winnipeg Guides • ⏱️ 8 min read • 📅 June 1, 2026

The Exchange District is one of Winnipeg's strongest areas for self-guided walking. The street grid is simple, block lengths are short, and architecture is visible from the sidewalk.

Route structure

Start near Old Market Square and move east-west across two or three parallel streets. This pattern lets you cover façades, galleries, and cafés without backtracking.

Architecture and history stops

Many buildings date to Winnipeg's warehouse era. Ground-level plaques and adaptive reuse details make the walk useful even without a formal tour guide.

Coffee and lunch break

Plan one café break and one meal break to avoid rushing. If you want evening options after your walk, see our cocktail bars guide.

Photo timing: Late morning gives even light on most street façades and reduces heavy shadow in narrow corridors.
