Winnipeg is an excellent destination for families, offering a wide range of activities that appeal to children and adults alike. Here are the best family-friendly attractions and experiences.

Top Family Attractions

Assiniboine Park Zoo

The Journey to Churchill exhibit is a must-see, featuring polar bears, seals, and arctic foxes in naturalistic habitats. The zoo also has a playground, splash pad, and train ride that kids love.

The Children's Museum

Located at The Forks, this hands-on museum encourages learning through play. Exhibits change regularly, ensuring there's always something new to discover.

Fun Mountain Water Slide Park

Perfect for hot summer days, this water park features slides, pools, and attractions suitable for all ages. There are areas specifically designed for younger children.

FortWhyte Alive

Explore nature through hiking trails, canoeing, and wildlife viewing. Kids can learn about bison, see beavers in their natural habitat, and participate in seasonal programs.

Outdoor Family Activities

The Forks

This historic site offers something for everyone: playgrounds, splash pads in summer, skating in winter, bike rentals, and plenty of space to run around. The market has kid-friendly food options.

Kildonan Park

Features a large playground, wading pool, walking trails, and the Rainbow Stage outdoor theatre. The park is perfect for picnics and family gatherings.

St. Vital Park

Great for families with playgrounds, walking trails, and open spaces. In winter, it's a popular spot for tobogganing.

Indoor Activities

Manitoba Museum

Interactive exhibits about Manitoba's history and natural environment. The Science Gallery and Planetarium shows are particularly popular with kids.

Royal Aviation Museum of Western Canada

Kids who love planes will be fascinated by the collection of aircraft and aviation history. Many exhibits are hands-on and interactive.

Rec Room

An entertainment complex with arcade games, virtual reality experiences, and activities suitable for older kids and teens.

Seasonal Family Fun

Summer

  • Festival du Voyageur (if visiting in February) - Winter festival with activities for all ages
  • Folklorama - Cultural festival with kid-friendly activities
  • Outdoor pools and splash pads throughout the city
  • Farmers' markets with family activities

Winter

  • Skating at The Forks or community rinks
  • Tobogganing at various city parks
  • Festival du Voyageur - Winter festival with activities
  • Indoor play centers and museums

Family Dining Recommendations

Many restaurants in Winnipeg are family-friendly. Look for places with kids' menus, high chairs, and a relaxed atmosphere. The Forks Market is particularly good for families, offering diverse food options that please everyone.

Family Travel Tip: Winnipeg's public transit system is family-friendly, and many attractions offer family passes or discounts. Check websites for current pricing and special offers before you visit.

Planning Your Family Visit

  • Pack for the weather - Winnipeg experiences all four seasons distinctly
  • Many attractions have stroller accessibility - check ahead if needed
  • Consider purchasing attraction passes if visiting multiple sites
  • Winnipeg is generally safe and welcoming to families
  • Look for hotels or accommodations with family amenities if staying multiple nights
