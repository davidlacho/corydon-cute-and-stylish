Red River Market: Winnipeg's Premier Farmers' Market

Every Saturday from May through October, The Forks transforms into a bustling marketplace as the Red River Market takes over the historic site. This vibrant farmers' market brings together local farmers, artisans, food vendors, and musicians, creating a weekly celebration of Manitoba's agricultural bounty and creative community. If you're visiting Winnipeg during market season, the Red River Market is an experience you won't want to miss.

What is the Red River Market?

The Red River Market is Winnipeg's premier outdoor farmers' market, featuring over 100 vendors selling everything from fresh produce to handmade crafts. The market operates every Saturday from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM, rain or shine, creating a weekly gathering place for locals and visitors alike.

What sets the Red River Market apart is its location at The Forks‚Äîone of Winnipeg's most historic and significant sites. The combination of fresh, local food, handmade goods, live music, and the beautiful riverside setting creates an atmosphere that's both festive and authentic.

What You'll Find

Fresh Produce

The heart of any farmers' market is the produce, and the Red River Market doesn't disappoint. You'll find:

  • Seasonal fruits and vegetables from local farms
  • Fresh herbs and greens
  • Manitoba-grown specialties
  • Organic and conventional options
  • Produce picked at peak ripeness for maximum flavor

Artisan Foods

Beyond fresh produce, the market features an impressive array of prepared foods and artisanal products:

  • Fresh baked goods, from bread to pastries
  • Local honey and maple products
  • Cheese and dairy products
  • Preserves, jams, and pickles
  • Meat and poultry from local farms
  • Prepared foods ready to eat

Handmade Crafts and Art

The market isn't just about food‚Äîit's also a showcase for local artisans:

  • Handmade jewelry and accessories
  • Pottery and ceramics
  • Textiles and clothing
  • Art and photography
  • Home decor and gifts

Live Entertainment

The Red River Market features live music throughout the day, adding to the festive atmosphere. Local musicians perform a variety of genres, from folk to jazz to contemporary, creating a soundtrack for your market visit.

The music, combined with the bustling activity, the smell of fresh food, and the beautiful setting, creates an experience that engages all your senses.

Seasonal Highlights

The market changes with the seasons, offering different products throughout the year:

  • Spring (May-June): Early greens, rhubarb, asparagus, and the excitement of the season's first produce
  • Summer (July-August): Peak season with tomatoes, corn, berries, and the full bounty of summer
  • Fall (September-October): Apples, squash, root vegetables, and harvest celebrations

Why Shop at the Market?

Shopping at the Red River Market offers benefits beyond just buying food:

  • Support Local - Your purchases directly support Manitoba farmers and artisans
  • Freshness - Produce is often picked within 24 hours of the market
  • Quality - Many vendors offer organic, pesticide-free, or sustainably grown options
  • Community - The market is a gathering place where you can meet farmers, artisans, and neighbors
  • Education - Vendors are happy to share information about their products and farming practices

Pro Tip: Arrive early for the best selection, especially for popular items. Bring cash, as not all vendors accept cards. Bring your own bags or baskets for carrying your purchases. Come hungry‚Äîthere are plenty of prepared foods to sample. And don't be afraid to ask vendors questions‚Äîthey love talking about their products!

Special Events

Throughout the season, the Red River Market hosts special events and themed markets. These might include:

  • Harvest celebrations
  • Chef demonstrations
  • Kids' activities
  • Seasonal festivals
  • Special vendor features

Check the market's website or social media for information about upcoming special events.

Getting There

The Red River Market is located at The Forks, making it easily accessible from our Airbnb in Crescentwood. The market runs every Saturday from May through October, from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Parking is available, and The Forks is also accessible by public transit.

The Market Experience

Visiting the Red River Market is more than just shopping‚Äîit's an experience. The combination of fresh food, handmade goods, live music, and the beautiful setting at The Forks creates a special atmosphere that's both festive and authentic.

You'll see families doing their weekly shopping, tourists discovering local products, musicians performing, and vendors sharing their passion for what they create or grow. It's a place where the community comes together, where you can taste the best of Manitoba, and where you can connect with the people who produce your food.

Why the Market Matters

The Red River Market represents the importance of supporting local agriculture and artisans. It provides a direct connection between producers and consumers, creating a more sustainable and community-oriented food system. The market also helps preserve farming traditions and supports small-scale producers who might not have access to larger retail channels.

For visitors, the market offers a chance to experience Manitoba's agricultural heritage and taste products that reflect the region's climate, soil, and growing conditions. It's a way to connect with the land and the people who work it.

Plan Your Visit

If you're staying with us during market season (May through October), make sure to visit the Red River Market. It's a perfect Saturday morning activity‚Äîgrab coffee, browse the vendors, pick up fresh ingredients for a meal, and enjoy the lively atmosphere.

The market is a celebration of local food, local art, and local community. It's a place where you can taste the best of Manitoba, meet the people who produce it, and experience one of Winnipeg's most vibrant weekly traditions. Whether you're looking for fresh produce, unique gifts, or simply a great way to spend a Saturday morning, the Red River Market has something for everyone.
