Planning a celebration, corporate event, or family gathering in Winnipeg? Whether you're organizing a wedding reception, birthday party, or business dinner, finding the right restaurant that can accommodate your group is essential. Winnipeg offers an impressive selection of restaurants with private dining rooms, large party capabilities, and catering services that can make your event memorable.

From intimate private rooms for 12 to grand venues that can host up to 1,000 guests, Winnipeg's dining scene has options for every occasion. Here's your complete guide to the best restaurants for group dining in the city.

Large Event Venues & Catering Services

Clay Oven Restaurant - Blue Cross Park

When it comes to large-scale events, Clay Oven Restaurant stands out as one of Winnipeg's premier catering destinations. They specialize in working with hotels and venues for weddings, parties, and special occasion dinners, accommodating groups from 25 people up to an impressive 1,000 guests.

Clay Oven provides comprehensive catering services at all temples in Winnipeg, making them a trusted choice for cultural celebrations and large gatherings. Their expertise in handling events of all sizes, combined with their authentic cuisine, makes them an excellent option for significant celebrations.

Capacity: 25 to 1,000 guests. Perfect for weddings, large parties, and special occasions. Catering services available at various venues throughout Winnipeg.

Fine Dining with Private Rooms

529 Wellington

For an upscale group dining experience, 529 Wellington offers an elegant setting in a renovated 20th-century mansion on one of Winnipeg's most prestigious streets. This tony steakhouse serves top cuts of Canadian beef prepared perfectly, along with market-fresh seafood.

The swank setting provides an impressive backdrop for business dinners, anniversary celebrations, or any occasion that calls for sophisticated dining. The refined atmosphere and impeccable service make it ideal for groups seeking a premium experience.

Bailey's Restaurant & Bar

A fine dining stalwart in Winnipeg's restaurant scene, Bailey's Restaurant & Bar is known for impeccable service and an elegant British-inspired atmosphere. Dark woods, deep rich tones, and a statement staircase leading to the upstairs dining room create a sophisticated setting.

The upstairs dining room is perfect for private group events, offering an intimate yet grand space for special occasions. Bailey's commitment to excellent service ensures your group will be well taken care of.

Hy's Steakhouse

This classic steakhouse on Portage Avenue serves top-drawer cuts including their famous "Only" steak with Hy's special sauce. The menu features porterhouse, filet mignon, New York Strip, and prime rib, making it a meat-lover's paradise.

Hy's elegant atmosphere and commitment to quality make it an excellent choice for business dinners, special celebrations, or any group that appreciates classic steakhouse dining.

SMITH

SMITH celebrates honest food created from the diversity of Manitoba's lands and waters. Built on craftsmanship and dedication to finer points, this restaurant offers fine fare done right. The menu showcases the bounty of local ingredients, making it perfect for groups who appreciate quality and local sourcing.

The Gates on Roblin

Located just west of the city limits in Headingly, The Gates on Roblin offers a pan-Canadian menu in a riverfront dining room. This upscale steakhouse features meat and seafood as the stars of the show, with a menu that changes seasonally to showcase the best ingredients.

The picturesque riverfront location adds to the appeal for group dining, making it a memorable choice for celebrations and special occasions.

Private Dining Rooms & Intimate Group Spaces

Nicolino's

Owner Nick Zifarelli has created a hip neighbourhood hangout in the south suburbs with rustic Italian classics that sparkle with innovation. Nicolino's features a private dining room perfect for 12 guests, ideal for luncheons, private parties, and intimate celebrations.

The combination of traditional Italian flavours with modern twists, plus the dedicated private space, makes Nicolino's a great choice for smaller group gatherings.

Peasant Cookery

A favourite amongst Winnipeg's downtown business and creative crowd, Peasant Cookery offers a dance between classic and modern French cuisine with a helping of down-home sense. House charcuterie is a must-try, and the restaurant's atmosphere makes it perfect for group dinners.

Located in the Exchange District, Peasant Cookery provides an excellent setting for business dinners, birthday celebrations, or any group looking for elevated comfort food in a welcoming atmosphere.

Rudy's Eat & Drink

This retro chic steakhouse is Mad Men incarnate, with classics like pot roast and meatloaf getting contemporary updates from talented chef/owner Michael Schafer. Chicken Cordon Bleu, signature salads, and other comfort food favourites make Rudy's a crowd-pleaser for groups.

The nostalgic yet modern atmosphere creates a unique dining experience that's perfect for celebrations and group gatherings.

Accessibility & Special Considerations

Many of Winnipeg's group-friendly restaurants offer wheelchair accessibility, though it's always best to confirm when making your reservation. Restaurants like Park Caf√© at Assiniboine Park and venues at The Forks are designed with accessibility in mind.

For groups with dietary restrictions or special requirements, most restaurants are happy to accommodate with advance notice. Whether you need vegetarian options, gluten-free alternatives, or specific cultural dietary requirements, Winnipeg's diverse restaurant scene can usually accommodate your needs.

Making Your Reservation

When planning group dining in Winnipeg, keep these tips in mind:

  • Book early: Private rooms and large party reservations often book weeks or months in advance, especially for popular dates and weekends.
  • Confirm details: Verify capacity, menu options, pricing, and any special requirements when making your reservation.
  • Consider timing: Some restaurants offer better group rates or availability during off-peak hours or weekdays.
  • Ask about catering: For very large groups, inquire about off-site catering options, which many restaurants offer.
  • Check parking: Confirm parking availability, especially for downtown locations, as this can be important for group events.
