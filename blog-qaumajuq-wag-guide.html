Qaumajuq and WAG guide: planning an Inuit art and gallery afternoon

📍 Winnipeg Guides • ⏱️ 8 min read • 📅 June 7, 2026

Qaumajuq is the Inuit art centre connected to the Winnipeg Art Gallery. It is a practical indoor attraction when you want a focused cultural stop in central Winnipeg.

How long to plan

Most visitors should allow 90 to 120 minutes for the main galleries. Add more time if you read full exhibition labels or visit special programming.

Ticket and timing notes

Check same-day admission and hours before leaving Corydon. Afternoon arrival can reduce crowding compared with weekend mid-morning blocks.

Pairing with nearby stops

After Qaumajuq, many visitors continue to The Forks or the Exchange District. See our The Forks guide for nearby planning.
