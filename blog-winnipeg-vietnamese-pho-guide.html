Winnipeg pho and Vietnamese dining in 2026

🍜 Winnipeg dining • ⏱️ 9 min read • 📅 May 11, 2026

Winnipeg's Vietnamese restaurant row clusters along Pembina Highway south of Corydon, but strong rooms also operate on Portage Avenue, Osborne Street, Ellice Avenue, and Notre Dame Avenue. Lunch specials rotate faster than websites update; call ahead for holiday closures common around Lunar New Year.

Pho Hoang

Pho Hoang lists four Winnipeg addresses: Sargent Avenue, Osborne Street, Portage Avenue in St. James, and Seasons of Tuxedo near Sterling Lyon Parkway. The group brands itself around modern dining rooms and traditional broth bases. Pick the branch that matches your day's driving loop.

Why go: Predictable branding, multiple neighbourhoods, and full menus beyond pho.

T.H. Dang Restaurant

T.H. Dang Restaurant on Pembina Highway advertises a wide menu of pho, vermicelli bowls, broken rice plates, and rolls. Family ownership since 2010 appears on the restaurant site; hours cover lunch and dinner most days.

Why go: Large menu for mixed groups and proximity to south Winnipeg shopping nodes.

Pho Kim Tuong

Pho Kim Tuong operates at 856 Ellice Avenue west of downtown. Listing pages show daily lunch and dinner hours with one weekday closure (confirm before visiting). The room suits guests touring the West End murals before heading toward broader restaurant corridors.

Why go: Central-west location and compact pricing relative to downtown dining.

Vi-Ann Restaurant

Vi-Ann sits at 1020 Notre Dame Avenue with posted hours extending past midnight on weekends in several listings. The kitchen mixes Vietnamese and Thai dishes; pho and grilled pork vermicelli appear on photographed menus circulating online.

Why go: Late hours when other broth kitchens close.

Plant-forward readers: See our Winnipeg vegan restaurant guide for dedicated meat-free rooms; Vietnamese menus vary widely on vegan broth accuracy, so ask staff directly.

Corydon connections

Guests at Corydon Cute & Stylish drive south on Pembina or east toward Osborne for the branches named above. Winter visits reward underground parking at Sterling Lyon sites when wind chill drops.

Stay nearby: Book Corydon Cute & Stylish for short hops to Osborne or Pembina Vietnamese kitchens.
