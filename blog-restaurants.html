Winnipeg's culinary scene has exploded in recent years, offering everything from innovative fine dining to beloved local institutions. Here's where to find the best meals in the city.

Fine Dining & Upscale

529 Wellington

An elegant fine-dining establishment known for its sophisticated menu and extensive wine selection. Perfect for special occasions.

Harth Mozza & Wine Bar

Harth Mozza & Wine Bar serves modern Italian cuisine with a focus on wood-fired pizzas and house-made pasta. The wine list is impressive, and the atmosphere is warm and inviting.

Peasant Cookery

Located in the Exchange District, Peasant Cookery focuses on rustic, farm-to-table cuisine with a French-Canadian influence.

Local Favorites & Casual Dining

Vera Pizza

Vera Pizza serves authentic Neapolitan pizza cooked in a wood-fired oven. This cozy spot in the Exchange District is a local favorite.

Famena's Famous Roti

For authentic Caribbean flavors, Famena's serves up delicious rotis and traditional dishes that have been a Winnipeg staple for decades.

Red Ember Common

Red Ember Common offers modern comfort food in a relaxed setting. Known for their burgers, poutines, and creative takes on classic dishes.

International Cuisine

Kum Koon Garden

Kum Koon Garden is one of Winnipeg's best dim sum restaurants, offering authentic Cantonese cuisine in a bustling, traditional setting.

Deer + Almond

Deer + Almond serves innovative small plates with global influences. The menu changes seasonally, and the cocktails are creative and delicious.

Thida's Thai

Family-owned Thida's Thai serves authentic Thai cuisine. The pad thai and curries are particularly popular.

Breakfast & Brunch

Stella's Cafe & Bakery

Stella's is a Winnipeg institution with multiple locations. Known for their extensive breakfast menu, fresh-baked goods, and strong coffee.

Marion Street Eatery

Marion Street Eatery is a popular brunch spot with creative dishes and generous portions. Be prepared to wait on weekends - it's worth it!

Vegetarian & Vegan Options

Affinity Vegetarian Garden

Extensive vegetarian and vegan menu with Asian-inspired dishes. Great for groups with diverse dietary needs.

Local Secret: Winnipeg is famous for its "Fat Boy" burgers and "Winnipeg-style" pizza (topped with cheddar cheese and pickles). Don't leave without trying these local specialties!

Food Markets & Food Halls

The Forks Market: Multiple food vendors offering diverse options in a historic setting.

Pembina Highway: This corridor is home to many excellent Asian restaurants, particularly Vietnamese and Korean cuisine.

Dining Tips

  • Many restaurants in Winnipeg are closed on Mondays - call ahead to confirm hours
  • Reservations are recommended for popular spots, especially on weekends
  • Winnipeg has a strong tipping culture - 15-20% is standard
  • Many restaurants offer happy hour specials on weekdays
  • Don't miss the local craft beer scene - Winnipeg has excellent breweries
