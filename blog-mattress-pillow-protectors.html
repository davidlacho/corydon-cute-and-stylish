Why Protection Matters

Accidents happen. Whether it's a spilled drink, a child's accident, or unexpected spills, your mattress and pillows are vulnerable. Without proper protection, a single incident can damage expensive bedding that's difficult or impossible to clean. For Airbnb hosts, this isn't just about protecting your investment‚Äîit's about maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness and hygiene for every guest.

Key Insight: Every guest deserves to sleep on a surface that's as clean as if it were brand new. Waterproof protectors ensure that even after accidents, your mattress and pillows remain pristine underneath.

Why We Replace Them Regularly

Here's the reality of hosting: accidents happen. A guest might spill a drink, a child might have an accident, or someone might get sick. When these things happen, we don't hesitate to replace the protectors. The cost of a new protector is minimal compared to the cost of replacing a mattress or dealing with stains that won't come out.

We keep spare protectors on hand at all times. When an accident occurs, we simply remove the soiled protector, replace it with a fresh one, and wash the soiled one. If it doesn't come completely clean or if we have any doubts about its condition, we replace it entirely. This ensures that every guest is sleeping on a protector that's in perfect condition.

Hosting Tip: Always keep at least one spare set of protectors for each bed. When accidents happen, you can immediately replace them and deal with cleaning later, ensuring minimal downtime between guests.
