Osborne Village and Corydon evening guide: patios, dessert, and walking routes

📍 Winnipeg Guides • ⏱️ 7 min read • 📅 June 5, 2026

Osborne Village and Corydon are close enough to combine in one evening. You can start with dinner in one area and finish with dessert or a drink in the other.

Start point options

Begin in Osborne for retail and café browsing, or begin on Corydon for patios and a shorter walk back toward Crescentwood.

Crossing at Confusion Corner

The key transition point is Confusion Corner. For evening trips, use signalized crossings and keep your route simple through the main intersection.

Food and dessert pacing

Leave 90 minutes for dinner service and 30 to 45 minutes for dessert or gelato. This schedule gives room for walking between neighbourhoods.

Late return: If weather changes, rideshare from Osborne to Corydon is typically short and avoids waiting at the intersection.
